How it works

Search for a book by title or author, or scan its ISBN barcode. Distill fetches cover art automatically, then calls your chosen AI to generate eight core learnings — one-sentence insights that capture the essence of the book. All data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any Distill server.

Bring your own API key

Distill works with your existing API keys from OpenAI, Anthropic (Claude), Google (Gemini), or Perplexity. Keys are stored securely on your device and never shared. You are responsible for your own usage and any associated costs. Each provider has a generous free tier or low-cost pay-as-you-go pricing — generating learnings for a single book costs a fraction of a cent.
