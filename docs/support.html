Frequently asked questions

How do I add a book?

Tap the + button in the Library tab. Search by title or author, scan the book's ISBN barcode, or type the details manually. Once you've selected a book, tap Generate with [Provider] to create your learnings.

Which AI provider should I use?

All four providers (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity) produce good results. If you don't already have a preference, OpenAI's gpt-4o-mini and Google's gemini-2.5-flash both offer generous free tiers. Claude and Perplexity require a paid account.

Where do I get an API key?

  • OpenAI: platform.openai.com
  • Claude: console.anthropic.com
  • Gemini: aistudio.google.com
  • Perplexity: docs.perplexity.ai

My API key shows as invalid.

Distill checks that your key has the right format (e.g. OpenAI keys start with sk-, Claude keys with sk-ant-). If the format looks right but you still get an error when generating, double-check that your key has credit available and hasn't expired in your provider's dashboard.

The widget isn't showing my books.

After adding a book, the widget should update within a few seconds. If it doesn't, try removing and re-adding the widget. Make sure Distill has been opened at least once after adding the book — the widget reads from shared storage that is written when you generate learnings.

How do I customise the widget?

Long-press the Distill widget on your home screen and tap Edit Widget. You can choose a colour theme (Indigo, Midnight, Sunset, Forest, or Rose), set how often the learning rotates, and toggle whether the book title and author are shown.

Can I delete a book?

Yes — tap a book in your Library to open its detail view, then tap the trash icon in the top-right corner. The book and all its learnings are removed immediately.

Does Distill work offline?

The library, review, and widget all work offline once books have been added. Generating new learnings and fetching book cover art require an internet connection.
