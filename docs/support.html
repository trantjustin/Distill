Frequently asked questions

How do I add a book?

Tap the + button in the Library tab. Search by title or author, scan the book's ISBN barcode, or type the details manually. Once you've selected a book, tap Generate Learnings to create your insights.

How much does Distill cost?

Distill offers a 7-day free trial, then $4.99 per month. The subscription gives you unlimited learnings and supports ongoing development. You can cancel anytime in Settings.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are handled securely through the App Store. No account is required. Your first week is free, then you are charged monthly until you cancel. You can restore a previous purchase in Settings.

The widget isn't showing my books.

After adding a book, the widget should update within a few seconds. If it doesn't, try removing and re-adding the widget. Make sure Distill has been opened at least once after adding the book — the widget reads from shared storage that is written when you generate learnings.

How do I customise the widget?

Long-press the Distill widget on your home screen and tap Edit Widget. You can set how often the learning rotates and toggle whether the book title and author are shown.

Can I delete a book?

Yes — tap a book in your Library to open its detail view, then tap the trash icon in the top-right corner. The book and all its learnings are removed immediately.

Does Distill work offline?

The library, review, and widget all work offline once books have been added. Generating new learnings and fetching book cover art require an internet connection.

Why am I seeing an error when generating learnings?

The most common causes are a lost internet connection or a temporary server issue. Make sure you have an active internet connection and try again. If the error persists, contact support with the book title and any error message shown.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → Apple Account → Subscriptions. Find Distill in the list to change or cancel your plan. You retain access until the end of the current billing period.

What AI is used to generate learnings?

Distill uses Groq to generate learnings. Only the book title and author are sent — no personal data or account information. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
